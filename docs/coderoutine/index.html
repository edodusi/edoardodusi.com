✨ Key Features

📖 Smart Reading Experience

  • Daily Curated Articles: One high-quality technical article delivered daily
  • AI-Powered Summaries: Generate intelligent summaries when you're short on time
  • Multi-Language Translation: Read summaries in Italian 🇮🇹, Spanish 🇪🇸, German 🇩🇪, or French 🇫🇷
  • Adjustable Reading: Customize font sizes and reading preferences
  • Distraction-Free: Clean, focused interface for optimal learning

🏆 Progress & Analytics

  • Reading Streaks: Track your daily learning consistency
  • Progress Visualization: Real-time reading progress with visual indicators
  • Tag-Based Analytics: Earn expertise points across different technology topics
  • Reading History: Complete history of your learning journey
  • Immutable Records: Once read, your progress is permanently saved

⭐ Organization & Favorites

  • Smart Favorites: Save articles you want to revisit
  • Categorized Content: Articles tagged by technology, framework, and topic
  • Quick Access: Easy navigation between current and past articles
  • Share Articles: Share interesting reads with your team

🚀 How It Works

  1. 📚 Daily Routine: Open CodeRoutine to discover today's coding article
  2. ⚡ Choose Your Mode: Read the full article or generate an AI summary
  3. 🌍 Your Language: Translate summaries to your preferred language
  4. 📊 Track Progress: Build your reading streak and earn topic expertise
  5. ⭐ Save Favorites: Bookmark articles for future reference

⏰ 24-Hour Article Cycle

Each article is available for 24 hours. After that, it expires and can no longer be marked as "read" (though you can still open it in your browser). This creates gentle urgency and helps maintain your daily routine.
